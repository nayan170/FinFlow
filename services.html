Home Loan

Turn your dream home into reality with our flexible home loan options

Our home loans are designed to help you purchase your dream home with ease. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer or looking to refinance, we offer competitive interest rates and flexible repayment terms.

Features & Benefits

  • Loan amounts from ₹5 lakhs to ₹5 crores
  • Competitive interest rates starting from 8.50% p.a.
  • Repayment tenure up to 30 years
  • Quick processing and approval
  • Minimal documentation
  • No prepayment charges

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21 to 65 years
  • Minimum income: ₹25,000 per month
  • Good credit score (preferably 700+)
  • Stable employment history

Required Documents

  • Identity & address proof (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID)
  • Income proof (Salary slips, Form 16, ITR)
  • Property documents
  • Bank statements for the last 6 months

Gold Loan

Quick cash against your gold assets with minimal documentation

Our gold loans provide you with quick access to funds by leveraging your gold assets. With minimal documentation and fast processing, you can get instant liquidity for your immediate financial needs.

Features & Benefits

  • Loan amounts from ₹10,000 to ₹50 lakhs
  • Competitive interest rates starting from 9.50% p.a.
  • Loan tenure from 3 months to 3 years
  • Quick disbursement within 30 minutes
  • Minimal documentation
  • No credit score check required
  • Multiple repayment options

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18 years and above
  • Must be the owner of the gold assets
  • Gold purity should be 18-22 carats

Required Documents

  • Identity proof (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID)
  • Address proof
  • Passport size photograph
  • Gold ornaments to be pledged

Property Loan

Leverage your property to secure funds for your various needs

Our property loans allow you to unlock the value of your existing property to meet your financial requirements. Whether it's for business expansion, education, or major expenses, we offer flexible loan options against your property.

Features & Benefits

  • Loan amounts from ₹10 lakhs to ₹10 crores
  • Competitive interest rates starting from 9.00% p.a.
  • Repayment tenure up to 15 years
  • Loan against residential or commercial property
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Part-payment and foreclosure allowed

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 25 to 70 years
  • Must be the owner of the property
  • Stable income source
  • Good credit score (preferably 650+)

Required Documents

  • Identity & address proof
  • Income proof
  • Property documents (title deed, tax receipts)
  • Bank statements for the last 6 months
  • Photographs of the property

Home Equity Loan

Tap into your home's equity for major expenses and investments

Our home equity loans allow you to access the equity you've built in your home. These funds can be used for home improvements, education expenses, debt consolidation, or any other financial need.

Features & Benefits

  • Loan amounts up to 80% of your home equity
  • Interest rates starting from 8.75% p.a.
  • Repayment tenure up to 20 years
  • Tax benefits on interest paid
  • Flexible repayment options
  • No restrictions on end-use of funds

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 25 to 70 years
  • Must be the owner of the property
  • Property should be fully constructed and habitable
  • Minimum income requirements based on loan amount
  • Good credit history

Required Documents

  • Identity & address proof
  • Income documents
  • Property ownership documents
  • Home valuation report
  • Existing mortgage details (if any)
